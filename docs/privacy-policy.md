# SimpliCalc Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

## Overview

SimpliCalc is an offline calculator application for Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how that information is used.

## Information We Collect

SimpliCalc does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, payment information, or account credentials.

SimpliCalc does not require account creation or sign-in.

## Calculation History

SimpliCalc stores your calculation log locally on your device so you can review previous calculations after closing the app.

This history is stored only on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

You can remove your saved calculation history at any time by using the `Clear all` option inside the app’s log screen.

## Clipboard Use

When you use the copy feature, SimpliCalc copies the visible calculation to your device clipboard.

This happens only when you explicitly tap the copy button.

## Data Sharing

SimpliCalc does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

## Data Retention

Any calculation history saved by SimpliCalc remains on your device until:

- you clear it manually from within the app, or
- you uninstall the app

## Children’s Privacy

SimpliCalc does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
